About
Roll.me is a social networking platform tool designed to revolutionize the routine practice of exchanging business cards and the difficult task of maintaining a professional rolodex. Endeavoring to liberate conventional networking ideals, Roll.me is effectively where business cards come to socialize because networking in the digital age should be just as innovative, empowering and exciting as technology has become for the world.

With core features allowing a user to create and exchange digital business cards that reflect the most up-to date information through digital synchronization of their rolodex, this mobile application helps reduce waste and eliminates the headache of shuffling through a stack of business cards for contact information of an acquaintance met weeks, months or years ago.

The application interface is intended to promote collaboration between diverse industry professionals and boost productivity by capturing multifaceted content in a single contact lessening the potential for sensory overload from a virtually unending media feed.
